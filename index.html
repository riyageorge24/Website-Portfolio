Summer Internship Projects

Project 1: Anti-Bow Cassette Stand

Mill: Galv 2

Mentor: Justin Speer

Description: Conducted on-field measurements and designed a stand for the Anti-Bow Cassettes to enhance safety and operational efficiency. Performed NASTRAN analysis to validate load-bearing capacity and strategically utilized existing inventory for material selection. The drawing created is in the approval process.

Project 2: #4 Furnace Structural Repair

Mill: HSM

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Calculated the center of gravity for an asymmetrical beam counterweight and verified its accuracy through simulation. Observed the entire project, interacting with riggers during the test lift and main lift phases. Ensured that calculations met CSI critical lift procedures and contributed to the installation of a 60 ft I-beam through effective teamwork and communication.

Project 3: FEA Analysis of Crane Hook

Mill: Galv 2

Mentor: Justin Speer

Description: Modeled and conducted NASTRAN analysis on a crane hook to assess its structural capacity in compliance with ASME BTH-1-2023 and ASME B30.20-2021 standards. Ensured the hook can safely handle a specified load of 6000 pounds.

Project 4: Forensic Evaluation and Modeling of Lifting Beam

Mill: HSF

Mentors: Justin Speer, Curtis Coombs & Pedro

Description: Conducted a non-destructive forensic evaluation using ultrasound testing to verify wall thickness and assess the structural integrity of the lifting beam. Created an as-built model on Inventor. Used NASTRAN for finite element analysis to evaluate the beam's performance while ensuring safety.

Project 5: Foundation Repair at Exit End of Build-up Line

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Conducted on-field measurements and contributed to foundation repair work at the exit end of the build-up line to restore structural stability and ensure safe working conditions.

Project 6: Debander Robot Stairway and Platform

Mill: HSF

Mentor: Curtis Coombs

Description: Designed on AutoCAD a new platform and staircase to facilitate the installation of the debander robot. Conducted field measurements, applied mill-requested changes, and provided structural calculations. Ensured compliance with building codes and OSHA requirements for platforms, stairs, handrails, guarding (fixed and removable), and fall protection tie-off points.

Project 7: Baffle Sensor Cover

Mill: Galv 2

Mentor: Justin Speer

Description: Modeled a cover for the baffle sensor to protect it from environmental damage and ensure accurate readings during operations. The drawing created is approved and on ENGWEB.
